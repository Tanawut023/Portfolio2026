# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
- Recruiters and hiring managers who need to assess fit quickly.
- Engineering leads who want a clear view of skills, experience, and project depth.
- Tanawut, when sharing the portfolio during a job search or interview.

## Product Purpose
This portfolio presents Tanawut Rungpetnimit as a Software Developer with insurance and enterprise delivery experience. It should help visitors understand his role, his technical strengths, the kinds of problems he has solved, and how to contact him with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality
Calm, precise, dependable.

The tone should feel professional and grounded, with enough personality to be memorable but never distracting. It should build trust quickly, communicate competence, and make the visitor feel that the work is organized, thoughtful, and ready for production.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS marketing pages that sell a product instead of presenting a person.
- Overly flashy portfolio templates that rely on motion, glow, or decoration to carry the experience.
- Costume-like developer aesthetics that feel gimmicky rather than credible.
- Dense pages that hide the essentials behind excessive scrolling or unclear hierarchy.
- Single-language presentation that forces one audience to work harder than the other.
- Overdesigned project cards that bury the actual impact of the work.

## Design Principles
- Lead with clarity, not spectacle.
- Make the page easy to skim in seconds, then easy to read in depth.
- Show proof of work through real experience, projects, and outcomes.
- Keep interaction patterns familiar and low-friction.
- Support bilingual reading without changing the underlying structure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive states.
- Keep the page fully usable with keyboard navigation and visible focus states.
- Support reduced motion so animation never blocks content.
- Make English and Thai equally legible and balanced in the layout.
- Avoid relying on color alone to communicate state or emphasis.
